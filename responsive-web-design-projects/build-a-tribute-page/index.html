Savage, Our Tortoise

Some Fun Details About Savage

  • We have had Savage for about six years now. He ran away when he was one-year-old and we almost lost him.
  • Up until this year, we thought Savage was a female.
  • Savage mostly eats lettuce.
  • One of Savage's favorite snacks is cherry tomatoes so be careful if you have your toenails painted red and are walking near him.
  • He will acknowledge you if you call his name and that's about the extent of his training.
  • He is our best boy.
